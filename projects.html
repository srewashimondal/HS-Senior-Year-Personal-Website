Rock Paper Scissors Game

Instructions for Extreme Rock Paper Scissors

1. Each player begins with 5 of each weapon.
2. Click on either the Rock, paper, or Scissor image under Player to make a move.
3. The computer will choose its weapon at random. Check the log on the bottom to see who won. An example of a move is that if I play rock and my opponent plays scissor, I win the round and add the scissor to my arsenal.
4. Remember that neither player may choose a weapon if it has exhausted its supply.
5. If a player plays a weapon more than 3 times in a row, that weapon “breaks”. It is removed from the game.
7. The winner of the round collects the opponent’s played weapon and adds it to their arsenal. In the event of a tie, each player recovers their own weapon.
8. The game ends when one player has all of the weapons.
